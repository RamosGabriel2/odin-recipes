Shrimp Tacos

Description

These Shrimp Tacos, in addition to allowing you to prepare something easy and quite fast, will not complicate you when acquiring the ingredients, since they are quite common; so you will have no excuses to give a special touch to that not so typical food, but Mexican. Mexican tacos have become popular all over the world, but have you tried them with shrimp? If you are one of those who plan themed meals but have already made more than one "Mexican dinner" we suggest this option so that you can surprise everyone with equally divine but more original tacos.


Ingredients

  • 800 grams of large shrimp
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 cups of chopped lettuce
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup of tomato puree
  • A bunch of fresh coriander
  • 2 jalapeno peppers
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 pinch of pepper
  • 1/4 cup tomato sauce
  • 8 corn tortillas

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. To make the tomato sauce you must mix the puree, the cilantro and the chilies.

  2. Step2

    3. Then for the special dressing mix the mayonnaise, the pepper and the tomato sauce.

  3. Step 3

    4. With the two sauces ready, peel the shrimp and then season them with salt and pepper.

  4. Step 4

    5. Place two bowls, one with the beaten eggs and the other with the ground bread.

  5. Step 5

    6. Pass each of the shrimp first through the egg and then through the bread.

  6. Step 6

    7. Then heat the oil in a pan over medium heat and fry the shrimp.

  7. Step7

    8. Once fried, place on paper towels to absorb the fat.

  8. Step 8

    9. Assemble the tacos by placing a corn tortilla, lettuce, dressing, sauce and shrimp.
